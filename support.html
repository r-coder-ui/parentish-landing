Frequently Asked Questions

How do I sign up?
Enter your phone number and we'll send a one-time code to verify your account. No email or password needed.
Is my phone number visible to other parents?
No. Your phone number is used only for sign-in and is never shown to anyone else in the app.
How do I post anonymously?
When creating a Vent post, toggle "Post anonymously." This feature is available with Parentish Pro.
What's included with Parentish Pro?
Pro includes unlimited posts, the ability to create events, the full meal planner, grocery lists, anonymous posting, and a Pro badge on your profile.
How do I cancel my subscription?
Subscriptions are managed through your Apple ID. Go to Settings → your name → Subscriptions on your iPhone or iPad to manage or cancel.
How do I delete my account?
Go to the Me tab in the app and tap "Delete my account" at the bottom. This permanently removes your profile, posts, and all associated data.
I found a bug or have feedback. How do I report it?
Use the contact form below — we read every message and appreciate the help.
