Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 13, 2026

Parentish ("we," "our," or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This policy explains what information we collect, how we use it, and your rights.

Information We Collect

  • Phone number — used for authentication only. Never shown to other parents or shared publicly.
  • First name and zip code — used to connect you with parents in your neighborhood.
  • Posts and replies — content you create in the app, associated with your profile.
  • Photos — profile photos and family photos you choose to upload.
  • Kids' ages — used to help match you with relevant local content and events.
  • Push notification token — used to send you notifications about replies and local activity.
  • Device information — basic device and OS info collected automatically for app performance.

How We Use Your Information

  • To authenticate your account via phone number (OTP)
  • To show you posts and events from parents in your zip code
  • To send push notifications about replies and local activity
  • To process subscription purchases via RevenueCat and Apple
  • To moderate content and keep the community safe

What We Don't Do

  • We do not sell your personal information to third parties
  • We do not show your phone number to other users
  • We do not use your data for advertising
  • We do not share your location beyond your zip code

Third-Party Services

  • Supabase — database and authentication (privacy policy)
  • RevenueCat — subscription management (privacy policy)
  • Apple — push notifications and in-app purchases (privacy policy)
  • Anthropic Claude — AI-powered content moderation and meal planning (privacy policy)

Data Retention

Your data is retained as long as your account is active. You can delete your account at any time from the Me tab in the app, which permanently removes your profile, posts, and all associated data.

Children's Privacy

Parentish is intended for parents and adults aged 18 and older. We do not knowingly collect information from children under 13.

Your Rights

You have the right to access, correct, or delete your personal data. Contact us at support@parentish.app.

Contact

Questions? Email us at support@parentish.app.
